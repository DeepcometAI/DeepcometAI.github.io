# The Aurelia Migration Forge

One of the primary barriers to adopting a new programming language is the massive amount of legacy code that organizations have already invested in. DeepcometAI addresses this through "The Forge"—powered by the **DeepComet-Code** model.

## Automated Migration

This specialized model is trained specifically on the Aurelia specification and millions of lines of C++ and Java. Its purpose is to facilitate the automatic migration of legacy codebases into Aurelia.

By reducing the transition cost for enterprise clients, The Forge accelerates the adoption of the DeepcometAI stack and ensures that organizations can leverage the "Speed of Light" performance of Aurelia without rewriting their entire software infrastructure from scratch.
