# SkyCloud

The SkyCloud model represents a strategic alternative to the centralized AI infrastructure of today.

## Decentralized Supercomputing

By utilizing the idle compute power of a global network of SkyOS devices, DeepcometAI can create a decentralized supercomputer. This not only provides a massive source of training data and compute power for the organization but also offers a more resilient and democratized alternative to traditional cloud providers.

## Self-Sustaining Ecosystem

The vision is a self-sustaining ecosystem where the hardware and software work in a symbiotic relationship to continuously improve the underlying AI models. Consumer products like the **SkyTablet**—a buttonless "Pane of Glass" running SkyOS—will act as nodes in this vast network.
